JS散度

发表于2022-12-29|更新于2022-12-29|DL
|阅读量:

$KL$散度的公式:
$$
KL(P||Q) = \sum P(x)log \frac {P(x)} {Q(x)} = \sum [ P(x)logP(x) - P(x)logQ(x) ]
$$
$$
KL(Q||P) = \sum Q(x)log \frac {Q(x)} {P(x)}
$$
设$ M = \frac { 1 } { 2 } (P + Q)$:
$$
JSD(P||Q) = \frac {1} {2} KL(P||M) + \frac {1} {2} KL(Q||M)
$$
蒋$KL$散度的公式代入得:
$$
JSD(P||Q) = \frac {1} {2} \sum P(x) log \frac {P(x)} {\frac {P(x) + Q(x)} {2}} +\frac {1} {2} \sum P(x) log \frac {Q(x)} {\frac {P(x) + Q(x)} {2}}
$$
$$
=\frac {1} {2} \sum P(x) log \frac {P(x)} {P(x) + Q(x)} + \frac {1} {2} \sum Q(x) log \frac {Q(x)} {P(x) + Q(x)} + log2
$$
当$P$和$Q$不重合或者重合部分非常小时:
$$
JSD(P||Q) = log2
$$
